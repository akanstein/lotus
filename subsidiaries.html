Mepe Farm GHLimited

It is an integrated farming company combining modern organic agronomic practices and technology with the goal and vision to become the largest agriculture production company in west Africa. We operate a Zero-Waste, Zero-Chemical Agricultural Entity.

Through longterm farming and processing off taker agreements with, we work together to enhance businesses, the agricultural value chain and food security. Our farming services are delivered in line with client specification and technical guidance.

Mepe Produce limited

using best-in-class greenhouse technology and a qualified all female agronomist team, we cultivate organic tomatoes and capscum annum under strict agronomist protocols.

Mepe Grains Limited

We are your go-to-source for the cultivation of grains and legumes. Under fully mechanized and organic conditions we cultivate raw materials to client specifications. Focus on Soya, Corn, Sesame Seeds and Teff.
