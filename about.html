About Us

Lotus Ecoculture is an agricultural development company utilizing arable farmland, innovative agronomic and agritech solutions and a fully-mechanized technological process to enhance local food production and exports in Africa.

Through Through strategic partnerships, we work with necessary stakeholders to aid employment, community development and Food Security.

We operate Zero chemical, Zero waste operations using ony OMRI (Organic Materials Review Institute) certified inputs, increase yields by over 30% and delivering world's best crops to our partners.

Our Goals

To be the go-to company for cultivation and processing services in Africa.

To own and operate a chain of efficient and cost-effective large scale farming and processing facility in Africa.

Using mechanization and biotechnology to achieve highest yields and quality organic crops setting a new standard in Africa.

Our Mission

To become a leading agricultural company operating farming and processing activities across West Africa.

Our Vision

To bring innovative organic and agronormic growing techniques to the market utilising 100% green farming methods.
